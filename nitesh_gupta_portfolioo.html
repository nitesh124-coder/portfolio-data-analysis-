Case Study

Retail Intelligence: Garment Store Analysis

Industry
Retail — Garment & Apparel
Scope
Full Inventory & Sales Intelligence
Data Points
8 Categories · 40+ SKUs · Daily Sales
Tools Used
Excel, Metabase, Power Query
View LinkedIn Profile →

A retail garment business needed clarity on which product categories were driving profit, which items were frequently out of stock, and where margins were being lost. I built an end-to-end data intelligence solution: structured the raw sales data, cleaned and validated records, built multi-view dashboards, and delivered actionable insights across all 8 product categories.



The analysis revealed that Men Wear drove the highest total profit (₹4.25L) while Outerwear was massively under-performing. Stock-out patterns showed Men Socks and Men Briefs had the highest average stock-out rates, directly costing revenue daily.
