Retail Garment Store
Garment Store — Revenue & Category Intelligence
Problem
A retail garment store had 40+ products across 8 categories but no visibility into which ones were actually profitable. The owner was making inventory decisions on gut feel.
Solution
Built a full revenue and profit dashboard breaking down performance by category and product — with margin analysis, stock-out tracking, and month-on-month comparisons.

Inventory Stock Analysis
Inventory Deep-Dive — Stock-Out Revenue Loss
Problem
The business was running out of high-demand items frequently — but had no data to show which SKUs, how often, or how much revenue it was costing.
Solution
Built a stock-out analysis dashboard tracking average stock-out per product, frequency, and correlation to profit loss — with a risk ranking of highest-impact SKUs.
Result
Men Socks (Pack) showed avg stock-out of 186 units — the #1 hidden revenue leak. Within 2 weeks, restocking priority was changed and the issue was measurably reduced.
